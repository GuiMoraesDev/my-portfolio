1. “We did X, it broke Y” (Failure → Insight)
   When to use
   something failed
   unexpected behavior
   performance regression
   bad DX decision
   Structure
   Title:
   We did X, and it broke Y

Context (2–3 lines)
What you were trying to do

What happened
Concrete description of the issue

Why it happened
The real reason (this is the value)

Fix
What you changed

Takeaway
1 strong sentence
Example ideas
“We optimized re-renders and made UX worse”
“We cached everything—and broke real-time updates”

👉 This builds credibility fast.
